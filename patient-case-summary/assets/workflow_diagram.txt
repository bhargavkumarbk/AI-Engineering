Patient Case Summary Workflow

[Patient Data (FHIR)] → [Data Extraction] → [Condition Mapping]
                                              ↓
[Case Summary] ← [Summary Generation] ← [Guideline Matching]

This diagram represents the flow of data through the patient case summary workflow:
1. Patient data in FHIR format is extracted into structured models
2. Conditions are mapped to relevant encounters and medications
3. Each condition is matched against clinical guidelines
4. A comprehensive case summary is generated for clinicians
